Oyster Crackers Recipe

Description

Oyster crackers coated in delicious ranch-style dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon dried dill weed
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 (12 ounce) package oyster crackers

Directions

  1. Mix together ranch dressing mix, garlic powder, dill and vegetable oil. Add crackers and mix gently until the crackers are coated with the mixture.
  2. Stir every 10 minutes for 1 hour. Store in an airtight jar.
